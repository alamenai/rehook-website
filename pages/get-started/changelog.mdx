I'm thrilled to share the first version of Rehook that includes mainly 16 standard custom hooks. 

All the hooks are under one category `hooks`, however in the future, I'll categorize them based on their usage.

Please feel free to try it and share your [feedback](https://github.com/alamenai/rehook/issues/new).
